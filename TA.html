Thanasis Antetekumpo

Thanasis Antetekumpo is the brother of Giannis Antetekumpo, who many consider the best player in the NBA. Thanasis was the 51st overall pick to the New York Knicks in the 2014 NBA Draft, and played 2 games for them during the 2015-16 season. He wasn't resigned after this, and many thought his NBA career was over. However, he was signed to the Milwaukee Bucks in the 2019-20 season to play with his brother. He was loved by the league and the Bucks, so although he was a pretty bad NBA player, he stayed on the team as their last roster spot. His effect is real, and it was shown greatly during the 2021 NBA Finals series vs. the Phoenix Suns. Although the Bucks went down 2-0, Giannis still had hope for the team, as he believed the only reason for this was that Thanasis didn't travel with the team for these two away games. With Thanasis on the bench, the Milwaukee Bucks came back and won the series 4-2, proving himself to be a key contributer off the court. He is still on the Bucks to this day, and although he has had endless lowlights and may be the worst player in the NBA based off of skill, he is a very respected and well-liked player.
