Killian Hayes

Killian Hayes was the 7th overall pick to the Detroit Pistons in the 2020 NBA draft. He was hyped up to be a star, but did NOT live up to the expectations. He started his career by averaging 7 points on 35% efficiency which wasn't great, but he was just a rookie at this point. Many expected a large improvement in his second year, but his stats were nearly the same. However, in his third year, he averaged 10 points and 7 assists. He still had pretty bad efficiency, but people had high expectations for 2024, as he was a starter. The first 3 games of his season went pretty well, as they went 2-1 over this stretch. However, this stretch was followed by a 28 game losing streak. He was one of the main reasons for this losing streak, leading him to get waived in February. The Pistons won their first game without him, showing the negative impact he had on the team. Killian Hayes is still struggling to find his spot in the NBA, and he plays for the Brooklyn Nets G-League team today.
