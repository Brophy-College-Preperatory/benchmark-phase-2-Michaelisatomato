Bronny James

Bronny James is the son of Lebron James. He was the 55th pick to the Los Angeles Lakers from the 2024 NBA draft. Many knew that he wasn't a very skilled player, as he only averaged 4.8 points in college. He would've likely gone undrafted if it wasn't for the fact that that his dad was on the team. Lebron and Bronny became the first father-son duo in NBA history, and they first shared the court in the Lakers second preseason game vs. the Phoenix Suns. He preformed very poorly in this game, and it stayed this way throughout the rest of the preseason. Although this switched in for last preseason game when he dropped 17 points, this has not converted to the NBA, as expected. He has barely played at all, and has played very poorly when on the floor. He was sent down to the G-League which will be good for some much-needed development, and will remain as a very popular player because of his dad.
